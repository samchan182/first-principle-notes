# The Real Time
Market price or last price is the price, which buyer wants to offer to buy, and seller wants to sell, both occurred in exchange's matching engine. 

How the Market Maker and HFT firms knows the 'last price'? They receive the total copy of orders from buyer and seller from exchange's matching engine. 

For example, in option trading, there is a 'OPRA', a law allows exchange (CBOE) to send orders to a single consolidated tape, it's like the official raw data feed. But also exchange also provide direct feed, to escape the consolidation part. 

For example, some HFT firms, they put their servers only 30 cm away from CBOE matching engine, which called 'colocation', they make money by the orders is faster that other parties ahead for few nanosecond. 

So they make money by creating the physical advantage, not digit (things like API...). 

Those companies make profit by the little cents of spread, and by doing this millions times per day. Someone offers 100 to sell, you buy it, and someone offers 100.02 to buy, and you give it to them.
